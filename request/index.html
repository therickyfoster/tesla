FAQ

Is winter planting really safer for soils?

With frozen ground and snow cover, access can reduce compaction and erosion. We measure and publish compaction baselines and adjust routes/tire pressure accordingly.

What about survival rates?

Trials compare myco-nests, warm-frames, and timing variants to quantify winter kill vs. spring vigor. Survival deltas are posted with data and methods.

How do you prevent wildlife conflicts?

Standoff protocols, quiet operations, no baiting, and post-visit cleanup‚Äîplus monitoring of any human/wildlife interface near fruit corridors.

Who can replicate?

All protocols and code are open. Educators, municipalities, and crews can fork the methods and adapt responsibly.
